About Rocket Squad

Rocket Squad represents Al-Khiar Secondary School in the prestigious Formula Pakistan competition. We are a team of young, passionate engineers and innovators dedicated to designing, building, and racing high-performance formula-style cars.

Under the leadership of Maryam Hashmat, our team combines technical expertise with creative problem-solving to compete at the highest level of student motorsports in Pakistan.
