IDARA Al-Khair(Our School)

Idara Al-Khair Welfare Society is a dedicated social welfare organization that has been serving communities in Pakistan since its establishment in 1987. For over 38 years, it has been committed to tackling critical issues such as poverty, illiteracy, and lack of healthcare through constant devotion and the support of philanthropists. The organization has established schools, a college, basic health centers, and vocational training centers, aiming to bring quality education to every child, regardless of their background.

The organization operates several core projects focused on essential human needs. These include providing access to basic healthcare through hospital wards and medical facilities, addressing hunger via initiatives like Roti Banks, and ensuring clean drinking water by installing wells in drought-affected areas like Tharparkar. Additionally, Idara Al-Khair runs an Orphan Care Program to provide a loving environment and engages the community through disaster relief and volunteer events, having served over 850,750 people to date.

Electronic Incorrect Engineering (EIE)

EIE (Electronic Interconnect Engineering) is a leading electronics engineering company based in Islamabad, Pakistan. We specialize in electronic product design, PCB development, hardware and firmware solutions, and electronic repairs. Our team also provides green energy solutions, power backup systems, and technical support for businesses and industries. At EIE, we combine innovation, precision, and reliability to deliver cutting-edge engineering solutions.

"At EIE, they’re not just creating electronics solutions — we’re empowering innovation, supporting emerging engineers, and shaping the future of technology in Pakistan."
