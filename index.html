About Our Team

Rocket Squad - Formula Pakistan

We are Rocket Squad, the Formula Pakistan racing team from Al-Khair Secondary School. Our team combines passion for engineering, innovation, and speed to compete in the prestigious Formula Pakistan competition.

Our mission is to design, build, and race a high-performance formula-style car while developing the next generation of engineers and motorsport enthusiasts.
